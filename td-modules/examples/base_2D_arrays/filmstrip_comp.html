Creating a Filmstrip Illusion

Similar to our previous example, here we also use a Texture3D TOP that's set to pre-fill. Here our instances are arranged based on a vertical line. The boarder dressing done in textures creates the illusion of a films strip. Finally, we use a Math CHOP to seamlessly snap our filmstrip back to the top at a regular interval.
